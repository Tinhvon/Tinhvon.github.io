Chapter5-04定积分的计算

定积分

定积分的计算

定积分换元积分法

第一种计算方法,与不定积分类似,是换元积分法,其表述如下:

设\(f(x)\)在\([a,b]\)上连续,作变换\(x=x(t)\)满足

  • \(x(\alpha)=a,x(\beta)=b\)
  • \(t\in[\alpha,\beta],x(t)\in[a,b]\)
  • \(x'(t)\in C[\alpha,\beta]\)

则\(\int_a^bf(x)\mathrm{d}x = \int_\alpha^\beta f[x(t)]x'(t)\mathrm{d}t\)

下面针对换元积分法,给出一个例题:

Example 1:计算\(\int_0^2{\sqrt{e^x}}\mathrm{d}x\)

Solution 作变换\(t = \cfrac{x}{2}\)

则\(I =\int_0^1 2 e^t \mathrm{d}t = 2(e-1)\)

定积分分部积分法

与不定积分相同,定积分也能通过分部积分法计算,方法如下:

\(\int_a^b{u(x)}\mathrm{d}v(x) = u(x)v(x)|_a^b-\int_a^bv(x)\mathrm{d}u(x)\)

如:

\(\int_0^1xe^x\mathrm{d}x = xe^x|_0^1-\int_0^1e^x\mathrm{d}x = 1\)

例题

最后来一些例题吧:

Example 1:已知\(\int_0^y e^t \mathrm{d}t-\int_0^x \cos t \mathrm{d}t=0\),求\(\cfrac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}\).

Solution 两边求导,有\(e^{y} y^{\prime}+\cos x=0 \Rightarrow y^{\prime}=-e^{-y} \cos x\)

Example 2:计算\(\cfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_1^xxf(t)\mathrm{d}t\)

Solution

\(\begin{aligned} & \cfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} x} \int_1^x x f(x) \mathrm{d}t=\cfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x} x \cdot \int_1^x f(t) \mathrm{d}t \\ & =\int_1^x f(t) \mathrm{d}t+x f(x) \end{aligned}\)

Example 3:计算\(\cfrac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} x} \int_0^x t\left(x^2-t^2\right) \mathrm{d}t\)

Solution 作代换\(x^2-t^2 = u\)

则原式变为$- _{x2}0 f(u) u=x f(x^2) $

Example 4:计算$_{x }

Solution 根据洛必达法则\(I =\lim\limits_{x \rightarrow 0} \cos x^2=1\)

Example 5:讨论\(f(x)\)在\(x=0\)处的连续性,可导性.

\(f(x)= \begin{cases}\cfrac{1-\cos x}{x} & x<0 \\ 0 & x=0 \\ \int_0^{x^2}\cfrac{\cos t^2\mathrm{d}t}{x} & x>0\end{cases}\)

Solution

\(\lim\limits_{x\to0^+}f(x) = \lim\limits_{x\to0}\int_0^{x^2}\cfrac{\cos t^2\mathrm{d}t}{x} = 0 =f(0)\)

\(\lim\limits_{x\to0^+}f(x)=\lim\limits_{x\to0^+}\cfrac{1-\cos x}{x}=0 =f(0)\)

故函数连续.

\(\begin{aligned} & f_+^{\prime}(0)=\lim\limits_{x\to0^+}\cfrac{f(x)-f(0)}{x}=\lim\limits_{x\to0^+}\cfrac{\int_0^x\cos t^2\mathrm{d}t}{x^2}=1 \\ & f_-^{\prime}(0)=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0^{-}} \frac{f(x)-f(0)}{x}=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{1-\cos x}{x^2}=\frac{1}{2} \\ & f_+^{\prime}(0) \neq f_{-}^{\prime}(0) \end{aligned}\)

故\(f(x)\)在\(x=0\)处不可导

Example 5:\(f(x)\)在\([0,2]\)可导,\(|f'(x)|\leq 1\),\(f(0)=f(2)=0\),求证:\(-1 \leq \int_0^2f(x)\mathrm{d}x \leq 1\).

Proof

根据柯西-施瓦兹不等式:\(\left|\int_0^2 f(x) d x\right| \leqslant \int_0^2|f(x)| d x=\int_0^1|f(x)-f(0)| d x+\int_1^2|f(x)-f(2)| d x\)

根据积分中值定理:\(RHS = \int_0^1 \mid f^{\prime}(\xi) \mid x d x+\int_1^2 \mid f^{\prime}(\eta)\mid (2-x) \mathrm{d}x \leq \int_0^1 x d x+\int_1^2(2-x) d x=1\)

Example 6:与定积分有关的极限

(1)计算\(\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(\cfrac{1}{n+1}+\cdots+\cfrac{1}{n+n}\right)\)

Solution

\(\begin{aligned} I & =\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(\frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{n}}+\cdots+\frac{1}{1+\frac{n}{n}}\right) \cdot \frac{1}{n} \\ & =\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\sum_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{1+\frac{k}{n}} \cdot \frac{1}{n} \\ &=\int_0^1 \frac{1}{1+x} d x \\ &= \ln 2 \end{aligned}\)

(2)计算\(\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left[\left(1+\cfrac{1}{n^2}\right) \cdots\left(1+\cfrac{n^2}{n^2}\right)\right]^{1 / n}\)

Solution 记\(a_n = \left[\left(1+\cfrac{1}{n^2}\right) \cdots\left(1+\cfrac{n^2}{n^2}\right)\right]^{1 / n}\)

则\(\ln a_n=\sum\limits_{k=1}^n \ln \left(1+\cfrac{k^2}{n^2}\right) \cdot \cfrac{1}{n}=\int_0^1 \ln \left(1+x^2\right) d x=\cfrac{\pi}{4}+\ln 2-2\)

故\(I = \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}a_n = 2e^{\pi/4-2}\)
