不定积分

不定积分的积分方法

在上一篇博文中,我们了解了不定积分的概念.但是我们对如何求解不定积分仍存在着不少困惑.这里我们将介绍一些积分方法.

公式法

公式法即利用基本函数的不定积分,以及积分运算的线性性.这里不多赘述,直接给出例题:

Example 1:求解\(\int[2^x-\sin x+2 \cos x+\cfrac{3}{1+x^2}-\cfrac{1}{x}+\cfrac{5}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}-x^2+\cfrac{6}{\cos ^2 x}-\cfrac{9}{\sin ^2 x}+1] \mathrm{d} x\)

Solution 利用基本函数的不定积分,可得:

\(I = \cfrac{2^x}{\ln 2}+\cos x+2 \sin x+3 \tan ^{-1} x-\ln x+5 \sin ^{-1} x -\cfrac{x^3}{3}+6 \tan x+9 \cot x+x+C\)

Example 2:计算\(\int \frac{x^4+1}{x^2+1} \mathrm{d} x\)

Solution

\(\begin{align}I &= \int \cfrac{x^4+1}{x^2+1} \mathrm{d} x \\&= \int \cfrac{x^4+2-1}{x^2+1} \mathrm{d} x \\ &= \int\left[\left(x^2-1\right)+\cfrac{2}{1+x^2}\right]\mathrm{d}x \\ &= \cfrac{1}{3} x^3-x+2 \tan ^{-1} x+C\end{align}\)

Example 3:计算\(I = \int \cfrac{\mathrm{d} x}{\sin ^2 x\cos ^2 x}\)

Solution

\(\begin{align} I &=\int \cfrac{\sin ^2 x+\cos ^2 x}{\sin ^2 x \cos ^2 x} \mathrm{d}x \\ &= \tan x-\cot x+C \end{align}\)

凑微分法(第一类换元积分法)

第一换元积分法很简单,其即是以下公式的应用: \[ \int{f[\varphi(t)]}\varphi'(t)\mathrm{d}t = \int{f[\varphi(t)]}\mathrm{d}\varphi(t) \] 在求解积分过程中,若发现函数的某些部分恰好是某些函数的导数时,可将其移至微分号内.

直接给出例题:

Example 1:计算\(\int \cfrac{d x}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}\)

Solution

\(\begin{align}I &= \int \cfrac{d\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)}{\sqrt{1-\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^2}} \\ &= \arcsin (\cfrac{x}{a})+C \end{align}\)

Example 2:计算\(\int \cfrac{d x}{a+x^2}\)

Solution

\(\begin{align} I &= \frac{1}{a} \int \frac{\mathrm{d} (\frac{x}{a})}{1+\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^2} \\ &= \cfrac{1}{a} \arctan (\cfrac{x}{a})+C \end{align}\)

Example 3:计算\(\int \sec x \tan x d x\)

Solution

\(\begin{align}I &= \int \cfrac{\sin x}{\cos ^2 x} \mathrm{d} x \\ &=-\int \frac{\mathrm{d} \cos x}{\cos ^2 x} \\ &= \sec{x} +C\end{align}\)

Example 4:计算\(\int \csc x \cot x \mathrm{d} x\)

Solution

\(\begin{align}I &=\int-\frac{\cos x}{\sin ^2 x}\mathrm{d}x \\ &=\int \frac{\mathrm{d}\sin x}{\sin ^2 x}\\ &=-\csc x+c \end{align}\)

Example 5:计算\(\int{\tan{x}}\mathrm{d}x\)

Solution

\(\begin{align}I &= \int{\cfrac{\sin x}{\cos x}}\mathrm{d}x \\ &= -\int{\cfrac{\mathrm{d}\cos{x}}{\cos{x}}} \\ &= -\ln{|\cos{x}|} + C\end{align}\)

同理我们有:

\(\int{\cot{x}}\mathrm{d}x = \ln{|\sin{x}|}+C\)

Example 6:计算\(\int \sec x d x\)

Solution

\(\begin{align} I &=\int \cfrac{1}{\cos x} d x \\ & =\int \cfrac{d \sin x}{\cos ^2 x} \\ &=\int \cfrac{d \sin x}{1-\sin ^2 x} \\ & =\cfrac{1}{2} \int \sqrt{1-\sin x}+\frac{1}{1+\sin x} d \sin x \\ & =\frac{1}{2}\ln| \frac{1+\sin x}{1-\sin x}|+C=\ln | \frac{1+\sin x}{\cos x} \mid+C \\ & =\ln| \sec x+\tan x \mid+C \end{align}\)

同理我们有:

\(\int{\csc{x}}\mathrm{d}x = \ln|\tan{\cfrac{x}{2}}|+C\)

Example 7:计算$ x$

Solution

\(\begin{aligned} I & =\int \cfrac{1+x}{x\left(1+x e^x\right)} d x \\ & =\int \cfrac{(1+x) e^x d x}{x e^x\left(1+x e^x\right)}=\int \frac{d\left(x e^x\right)}{\left(x e^x\right)\left(1+x e^x\right)} \\ & =\int\left[\cfrac{1}{x e^x}-\cfrac{1}{1+x e^x}\right] d\left(x e^x\right) \\ & =\ln \left|\cfrac{x e^x}{1+x e^x}\right|+C \end{aligned}\)

第二类换元积分法

针对积分\(\int{f(x)}\mathrm{d}x\),若我们作代换\(x=x(t)\),此时积分变为\(\int{f[x(t)]}x'(t)\mathrm{d}t\).且后者积分更容易求出.我们称这种换元方法为第二类换元积分法.

下面我们给出几个例题:

Example 1:计算:$ $

Solution 作代换\(t=\sqrt[6]{x} \Rightarrow x = t^6\)

\(\begin{aligned} I &= \int \cfrac{6 t^3 \mathrm{d} t}{t+1} \\ &=6 \int\left(\cfrac{t^3+1}{t+1}-\frac{1}{t+1}\right) \mathrm{d} t \\ &= 2\sqrt{x}-3\sqrt[3]{x}+6\sqrt[6]{x}-6\ln{(1+\sqrt[6]{x})}+C \end{aligned}\)

Example 2:计算$ $

Solution 作代换\(x=a \tan t\)

\(\begin{aligned}I & =\frac{1}{a^2} \int \frac{\sec ^2 t}{\sec ^3 t} \mathrm{d}t=\frac{1}{a^2} \int \cos t \mathrm{d}t \\ &= \cfrac{1}{a^2}\sin{t}+C \\ &= \cfrac{1}{a^2}\cfrac{x}{\sqrt{x^2+a^2}}+C \end{aligned}\)

Example 3:计算$ $

Solution 作代换$x=a t $

\(\begin{aligned} I & =\frac{1}{a^2} \int \frac{\sec t \tan t}{\tan ^3 t} \mathrm{d}t=\frac{1}{a^2} \int \frac{\cos t}{\sin t} \mathrm{d}t \\ & =-\frac{1}{a^2} \frac{1}{\sin t}+C=-\frac{1}{a^2} \frac{x}{\sqrt{x}-a^2}+C \end{aligned}\)

Example 4:计算\(\int \cfrac{\mathrm{d}x}{\left(a^2-x^2\right)^{\frac{3}{3}}}\)

Solution 作代换\(x=a \sin t\) \(\begin{aligned} I & =\frac{1}{a^2} \int \frac{\cos t}{\cos ^3 t} d t=\frac{1}{a^2} \tan t+C \\ & =\frac{1}{a^2} \frac{x}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}+C \end{aligned}\)

Example 5:计算$ $

Solution 作代换\(x=a \sinh t\)

\(\begin{aligned} I & =\int \mathrm{d}t=t+C=\ln \left(x+\sqrt{x^2+a^2}\right)+C \end{aligned}\)

Example 6:计算$ $

Solution 作代换\(x=a \cosh t\) \(\begin{aligned} I & =\int d t=t+C=\ln\left|x+\sqrt{x^2-a^2}\right|+C \end{aligned}\)

分部积分法

分部积分法基于导数的乘积公式\((uv)' = u'v+uv'\).对乘积公式两边积分,则有\(uv = \int{v}\mathrm{d}u + \int{u}\mathrm{d}v\).

移项后,我们有: \[ \int{u}\mathrm{d}v = uv- \int{v}\mathrm{d}u \] 此即分部积分法的内容,下面我们给出一些例题:

Example 1:计算\(\int x e^x \mathrm{d}x\)

Solution

\(\begin{aligned} I &=\int x \mathrm{d}e^x\\ &=x e^x-\int e^x d x \\ & =x e^x-e^x+C \end{aligned}\)

Note:这种方法可以求解类似于\(\int{p(x)e^{\alpha x}}\mathrm{d}x\)的积分.

Example 2:计算\(\int x \cos x d x\)

Solution

\(\begin{aligned}I &= \int x \mathrm{d}\sin x \\ &=x \sin x -\int \sin x \mathrm{d}x \\ &=x \sin x+\cos x+C \end{aligned}\)

Note:这种方法可以求解类似于\(\int{p(x)\cos{x}}\mathrm{d}x,\int{p(x)\sin{x}}\mathrm{d}x\)的积分.

Example 3:计算\(\int 2 x \ln x d x\)

Solution

\(\begin{aligned} I &=\int \ln x \mathrm{d}x^2 \\ &=x^2 \ln x-\int x\mathrm{d}x \\ &=x^2 \ln x-\cfrac{x^2}{2}+C \end{aligned}\)

Example 4:计算\(\int e^x \sin x d x\)

Solution \(\begin{aligned}I &= \int \sin x \mathrm{d}e^x \\ &=e^x \sin x-\int e^x \cos x \mathrm{d}x \\ &=e^x \sin x-\int \cos x \mathrm{d}e^x \\ &=e^x \sin x-e^x \cos x-\int e^x \sin x \mathrm{d}x\\ & \Rightarrow\int e^x \sin x \mathrm{d}x=\cfrac{1}{2} e^x(\sin x-\cos x)+C \end{aligned}\)

同理可以求出$x = e^x(x+x)+C $
