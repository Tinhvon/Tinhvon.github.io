微积分-Chapter1-极限与连续（Part 3）

极限与连续 (Part 3)

例题

最后我们给出一些极限的例题吧！

exp1:求极限\(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}[\sqrt[3]{(x+a)(x+b)(x+c)}-x]\).

Solution:

\(\begin{align}I &=\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\cfrac{[\sqrt[3]{(1+\frac{1}{x})(1+\frac{b}{x})(1+\frac{c}{x})}-1]}{\frac{1}{x}} \\ &= \lim\limits_{t \to 0}\cfrac{[\sqrt[3]{(1+t)(1+t)(1+t)}-1]}{t}\\&=\lim\limits_{t \to 0}\cfrac{\sqrt[3]{1+(a+b+c)t+o(t)}-1}{(a+b+c)t+o(t)}\cdot \cfrac{(a+b+c)t+o(t)}{t} \\ &= \cfrac{1}{3}(a+b+c)\end{align}\)

exp2:求极限\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}\cfrac{x^x-(\sin x)^x}{x^2\arctan x}\)

Solution:

\(\begin{align}I &=\lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}(-x^x)\cfrac{(\frac{\sin x}{x})^x-1}{x^3} \\ &= \lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}\cfrac{e^x\ln{\frac{\sin x}{x}}-1}{-x^3} \\ &= \lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}-\cfrac{\ln{\frac{\sin x}{x}}}{x^2} \\ &= \lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}-\cfrac{\ln({1+\frac{\sin x-x}{x}})}{x^2} \\ &= \lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}-\cfrac{\sin x-x}{x^3} \\ &= \cfrac{1}{6}\end{align}\)

exp3:已知数列满足递推关系:\(x_1=a,x_2=b,x_{n+2}=\cfrac{x_{n+1}+x_{n}}{2},\)求\(\lim\limits_{n \to \infty }x_n\)

Solution:化简递推关系,有:

\(x_{n+2}-x_{n+1} =-\cfrac{1}{2}(x_{n+1}-x_{n}) = \cfrac{1}{2^2}(x_n-x_{n-1}) = ... = (-1)^{n}\cfrac{1}{2^n}(b-a)\)

同理有\(x_{n+1}-x_{n} = (-1)^{n-1}\cfrac{1}{2^{n-1}}(b-a)\)

故有\(x_{n+2}-x_{1} = (1-\cfrac{1}{2}+\cfrac{1}{2^2}+...+(-1)^n\cfrac{1}{2^n})(b-a)\)

故\(\lim\limits_{n \to \infty }x_n = a+\cfrac{2(b-a)}{3}= \cfrac{a+2b}{3}\)

同理,若exp3条件改为\(x_1=a,x_2=b>0,x_{n+2} = \sqrt{x_nx_{n+1}}\),则同理可求\(\lim\limits_{n\to \infty}x_{n} = \sqrt[3]{ab^2}\)

exp4:已知数列\(\{x_n\}\)满足\(0<x_1<\pi,x_{n+1}=\sin{x_n}\)

  • 证明:\(\lim\limits_{n \to \infty}x_n\)存在并求其值；
  • 求\(\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}(\cfrac{x_{n+1}}{x_{n}})^{1/x_n^2}\)

Solution:

  • 我们考虑用归纳法证明\(\{x_n \}\)单调有界:

​ 当\(n=1\)时,有\(0<x_2 = \sin{x_1} \leq x_1<\pi\)

​ 设\(0<x_n<\pi\)时,有\(0<x_{n+1}=\sin{x_n}\leq x_{n}< \pi\)

​ 故\(\{x_n \}\)单调递减且有下界\(\Rightarrow\lim\limits_{n \to \infty}x_n\)存在,且设其为\(a\),故有\(a = \sin{a} \Rightarrow a=0\)

\(\begin{aligned}I&= \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}(\cfrac{x_{n+1}}{x_n})^{1/x_n^2} \\&= \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}(\cfrac{\sin{x_n}}{x_n})^{1/x_n^2} \\ &= \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}(1+\cfrac{\sin{x_n}-x_n}{x_n})^{x_n/(\sin{x_n}-x_n)\cdot (\sin{x_n}-x_n)/x_n^3} \\ &= e^{-\frac{1}{6}}\end{aligned}\)

exp5:函数\(f(x)\in C[a,b],x_i \in [a,b],\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\lambda_i=1(\lambda_i > 0)\),求证\(\exists \xi \in [a,b]\),使得\(f(\xi) = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\lambda_if(x_i)\)

Proof:设函数在\([a,b]\)上的最大值为\(M\),最小值为\(m\)

则有\(m \leq \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\lambda_if(x_i) \leq M\)

由介值定理:\(\exists \xi \in [a,b]\),使得\(f(\xi) = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}\lambda_if(x_i)\)

exp6:设\(f(x) \in C[0,n],f(a) =f(n)\),求证:\(\exists \xi \in (0,n-1)\),使得\(f(\xi +1)=f(\xi)\)

Proof:构造\(F(x)=f(x+1)-f(x),x\in[0,n-1]\)

\(F(k)=f(k+1)-f(k) \Rightarrow F(0)+F(1)+...+F(n-1)=f(n)-f(0)=0\)

\(\exists \xi \in (0,n-1),\Rightarrow F(\xi) = \cfrac{F(0)+F(1)+...+F(n-1)}{n} = 0\),即\(f(\xi +1) = f(\xi)\)

exp7:\(f(x)\in(a,+\infty),\lim\limits_{x\to a^+}f(x)=A,\lim\limits_{x\to+\infty} = B,AB<0\),求证\(\exists \xi \in (a,+\infty),f(\xi) =0\)

Proof:不妨设\(A < 0,B > 0,\lim\limits_{x\to+\infty} = B\),根据极限的保号性可知\(\exists x_0 > a \Rightarrow f(x_0) > 0\)

在\((a,x_0)\)上,根据零点存在性定理,易知\(\exists \xi \in (a,+\infty),f(\xi) =0\)
